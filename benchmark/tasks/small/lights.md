# Traffic light

Build a traffic light: three stacked labels "RED", "AMBER", "GREEN" of which
exactly one is highlighted (bold/large) at a time, plus a "Next" button that
advances RED → GREEN → AMBER → RED, and an "Auto" toggle button that advances
automatically every second while on.

Acceptance:
- Next cycles in the stated order.
- Auto advances once per second; toggling Auto off stops it.
- Manual Next still works while Auto is off.
